Frequently Asked Questions

Browse our most frequently asked questions list below to learn everything you need to know!

Are your rentals clean?

Absolutely! We take cleanliness very seriously. Each inflatable rental undergoes our thorough 3-step cleaning process after every use:

  1. Sweep: We remove all dust, dirt, and debris
  2. Deep Clean: We thoroughly clean each unit with cleaner and sanitizer
  3. Final Check: We ensure everything is clean, sanitized, and ready for fun!

We pride ourselves in providing clean and safe rentals. Each unit is cleaned and sanitized after every rental to guarantee your safety and satisfaction.

Do your units contain any lead in the vinyl and are they safe?

Safety is our top priority. All our bounce houses and inflatables are made with commercial-grade, lead-free vinyl that meets or exceeds all safety regulations and standards. We regularly inspect our equipment to ensure it remains in excellent condition for your safety.

We absolutely will not sacrifice your safety for business. EVER. All our products undergo regular maintenance checks and are cleaned thoroughly between each use.

What type of parties and events do you provide rentals for?

We provide rentals for virtually all types of events! Our inflatable rentals are perfect for:

  • Birthday parties
  • School events and fundraisers
  • Corporate events
  • Community festivals
  • Church events
  • Block parties
  • Family reunions
  • Grand openings and sales events (especially our Sky Dancers)
  • And many more!

Whether it's in your backyard or a huge corporate event, we specialize in making sure your event is a fun, stress-free experience from start to finish.

Can I use a water hose on a bounce house?

Unless specifically designated as a water slide or water bounce house, we do not recommend using water with our standard bounce houses. Water can make the jumping surface slippery and potentially dangerous.

If you're interested in a water feature for your event, please ask about our specialized water inflatables that are designed for safe water play. These units are specifically designed with proper drainage and slip-resistant surfaces.

Does the rental have to be set up on grass?

Our inflatables can be set up on grass, cement, concrete, asphalt, or even indoors with a different anchoring system available for every possible combination. When placing your reservation, please let us know on what type of terrain your rental will be installed.

Inflatables that are setup on grass are anchored with long steel stakes, while inflatables that are setup on other surfaces or indoors are anchored with sandbags.

Still have questions?

Feel free to reach out to us anytime. We're here to help make your event planning as easy and enjoyable as possible!
