BOUNCE BOSS Rental Agreement

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions for renting inflatable equipment from BOUNCE BOSS. By agreeing to these terms, you acknowledge and accept the following:

1. Safety Guidelines

  • Adult supervision is required at all times
  • No food, drinks, or shoes inside the inflatable
  • No flips, rough play, or climbing on walls
  • No sharp objects, jewelry, or glasses
  • Maximum capacity must be observed
  • Children of similar age and size should play together

2. Weather Policy

BOUNCE BOSS reserves the right to cancel any booking due to inclement weather conditions (heavy rain, winds exceeding 15mph, lightning, or extreme temperatures). In such cases:

  • BOUNCE BOSS will offer to reschedule your event
  • If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund will be provided
  • For customer cancellations due to weather, 24-hour notice is required for full refund

3. Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations with 7+ days notice: Full refund
  • Cancellations with 3-6 days notice: 50% refund
  • Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice: No refund
  • Booking date changes require 48-hour notice and are subject to availability

4. Setup Requirements

  • Customer must provide a flat, clear area of appropriate size for the inflatable
  • Area must be free of rocks, sticks, sprinklers, and other sharp objects
  • A power source within 100 feet of setup location is required
  • Customer is responsible for obtaining any necessary permits or HOA approvals

5. Liability

The customer assumes all responsibility and liability for any injury or damage to persons or property arising from the use of the rental equipment. BOUNCE BOSS is not responsible for accidents or injuries occurring during the use of rental equipment.

6. Damage to Equipment

Customer is responsible for all damage to equipment beyond normal wear and tear. Repair or replacement costs will be charged to the customer's credit card on file.

7. Additional Costs

Additional fees may apply for excessive cleaning requirements, late returns, or setup locations beyond the agreed service area.
